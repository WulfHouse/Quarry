# Authors

## Creator and Original Developer

**Alexander Rose Wulf** (also known as **Aeowulf** and **WulfHouse**) - Creator and sole developer of the Pyrite programming language and the Quarry SDK.

**Official Aliases:**
- **Aeowulf** - Official alias for Alexander Rose Wulf
- **WulfHouse** - Official alias for Alexander Rose Wulf

**Pyrite** was first invented on December 12, 2025. **Forge** is the Pyrite compiler. **Quarry** is the official Pyrite SDK that includes Forge, build system, package manager, language server, and all development tools.

All code, documentation, and design decisions in this project were created by Alexander Rose Wulf (Aeowulf/WulfHouse) unless otherwise noted.
